Head in the Clouds

When I was a little girl

I would look up at the clouds and see

a fleet of old fashioned sailing ships,

a pile of jewels, or

a floating tree house

filled with other kids having adventures.

I saw so many wonderful things.

And even though I know better now,

even though I have gone to school

and learned about water vapor

and weather patterns,

even though I am a grown up,

when I look at the clouds I still think:

Castle.

Leah Rose Marks
