Head in the Clouds

When I was a little girl

I would look up at the clouds and see a fleet of old fashioned sailing ships.

A pile of jewels.

A floating tree house with children having adventures.

I saw so many wonderful things.

And even though I know better now,

even though I have gone to school and learned about water vapor and weather patterns,

even though I am a grown up, when I look at clouds I still think:

castle.
