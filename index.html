Head in the Clouds

When I was a little girl

I would look up at the clouds and see

a fleet of old fashioned sailing ships,

a pile of jewels, or

a floating tree house

filled with other kids having adventures.

I saw so many wonderful things.

And even though I know better now,

even though I have gone to school

and learned about water vapor

and weather patterns,

even though I am a grown up,

when I look at the clouds I still think:

Castle.

Leah Rose Marks

Super

If you could have any super power in the world, what would it be? I spent a while thinking about the power of imagination and what would happen if I had a character who could make anything happen. A character who was only limited by their own imagination. What challenges could possibly stand in their way? What would I do with such a power? "Super" started out as an assignment for an introductory Creative Writing course in the summer of 2011, and has turned into a fun exercise in the power of imagination. What do you think will happen next?
